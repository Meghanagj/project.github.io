Honnavar

Honnavar is famous for its pristine beaches, stunning waterfalls, historical temples, and lush greenery. Key attractions include Eco Beach, Apsarkonda Waterfalls, and Basavaraj Durga. It is a perfect blend of nature, history, and culture.

Nanjangud

Srikanteshwara Temple is a prominent Hindu temple located in the city of Nanjangud. This temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is believed to be one of the oldest temples in Karnataka.

Dandeli

Dandeli is an adventure sports destination, and a white-water rafting destination in South India. Dandeli also offers many other adventure activities like Kayaking, Zorbing, Jungle safari, Cannoeing, River crossing etc.
